Naperville Central CS Club

What Does CS Club Do?

CS Club provides a space for students to have a personalized space for programming! We provide students with workshops that are student led and give members the opportunity to have tutoring on a given topic. We also provide students with coding prompts ranging from easy to hard. By doing coding prompts, students enter a raffle where they can win different tech related prizes!

Club Details

When do we meet?

Every week on Monday and Tuesday morning

What room?

CS Club meets at room 46

What workshops/projects is CS Club currently involved in?

Python, Data Analysis, AI, Flutter (app dev), a project that helps the school with SOAR automation, and a project based on running /maintaining the Central Times App

Can CS Club work be done asynchronously?

Yes! Our projects as well our workshops (with some exception) can be done asynchronously or as instructed by the tutor.

Do you need experience to join?

No! You can join CS Club regardless of skill level because we have offerings that appeal to beginners, intermediate, and even advanced coders.
